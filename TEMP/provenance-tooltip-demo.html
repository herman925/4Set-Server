✨ Tooltip Features:

  • Grade context - Shows which grade level (K1/K2/K3) the data belongs to
  • Source counts - Number of JotForm submissions and Qualtrics responses checked
  • Identifiers - Submission IDs, session keys, response IDs with timestamps
  • Winner explanation - Why a specific source was chosen (earliest non-empty, conflict resolution)
  • Accessible - Works with keyboard (Tab, Escape), screen readers, and touch devices
